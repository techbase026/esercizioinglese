Past Continuous

Esercizio interattivo: affermativa, negativa, interrogativa

Parte A - Completa con was / were e il verbo in -ing

  1. I (watch) TV at 9 p.m.
  2. They (play) in the park.
  3. She (not / study) for the test.
  4. We (have) dinner.
  5. you (sleep) at midnight?

Parte B - Trasforma le frasi

  1. She was reading a book. -> (negativa)
  2. They were playing football. -> (interrogativa)
  3. I wasn't working. -> (affermativa)

Parte C - Scegli la forma corretta

  1. While I dinner, the phone rang.
  2. They at 8 p.m.

Soluzioni

Parte A

  1. I was watching TV at 9 p.m.
  2. They were playing in the park.
  3. She was not studying / wasn't studying for the test.
  4. We were having dinner.
  5. Were you sleeping at midnight?

Parte B

  1. She was not reading a book. / She wasn't reading a book.
  2. Were they playing football?
  3. I was working.

Parte C

  1. While I was eating dinner, the phone rang.
  2. They were studying at 8 p.m.
